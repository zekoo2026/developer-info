# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

This App developer (hereinafter "Operator") establishes this Privacy Policy to define the handling of personal information and usage data in the Apps and related services (hereinafter "Services").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information (OS version, device model, language settings)
- Usage data (screen views, operation logs, crash reports)
- **Firebase Analytics** data
- **Google AdMob** advertising data and click information
- Advertising ID

### User Input and Purchase Information
- In-app purchase history (actual payment information is managed by Google/Apple)
- User settings and saved data

## 2. Purpose of Use
- Providing and improving the Services
- Analyzing usage with **Firebase Analytics**
- Delivering appropriate advertisements via **Google AdMob**
- Managing in-app purchases and preventing fraud
- Sending important notifications

## 3. Sharing with Third Parties
We share information with the following services:
- **Google Firebase** (Analytics): https://firebase.google.com/terms/analytics
- **Google AdMob** (Advertising): https://admob.google.com/home/terms/

These services process data in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

## 4. Advertising
The Apps display advertisements using **Google AdMob**.  
Ads may be personalized based on your interests and behavior.  
You can reset your Advertising ID in your device settings.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal information
- Withdraw consent or request cessation of data processing
- Opt out of personalized advertising

To exercise these rights, please contact us via the [Contact page](./contact.md).

## 6. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. We will notify users of any changes on this page.
